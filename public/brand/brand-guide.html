Usage Rules

Do
  • Use the provided logo files without modification
  • Maintain minimum clear space around the logo
  • Use teal-on-light for light backgrounds
  • Use white or mint-on-dark for dark backgrounds
  • Keep the wordmark lowercase
Don't
  • Rotate, skew, or distort the logo
  • Change the logo colors
  • Add drop shadows, outlines, or effects
  • Place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Capitalise the wordmark ("GrubShelf" as logo text)

Voice & Tone

GrubShelf speaks like a sharp, helpful friend — direct, warm, and a little witty. Never corporate. Never condescending.

Principles

Conversational
You walk into a store and forget everything you came for. So you guess. You overspend. You waste food.
Honest & Direct
Bring the list back — without the paper, the panic, or the walk of shame past the bakery twice.
Warm & Supportive
A short email every week or so. Food hacks that actually work, recipes worth keeping, and the kind of stuff that rescues that cilantro you forgot about.
Action-Oriented
Plan smarter. Shop better. Waste less.
